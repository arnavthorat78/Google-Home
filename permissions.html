Permissions

This page needs access to permissions. Use the following steps to allow the permissions.
If you don't know how to enable permissions, follow the steps below. However, if you do know, enable the setting Pop-ups and redirects yourself.

Important Notice: Since version 1.1.0 is currently pending, some features of this website may not work properly.

To enable pop-ups and redirects, preform the following steps depending on your browser:

For Google Chrome:
    1. Navigate to the button next to the URL (site information).
    2. On the bottom, click Site Settings.
    3. Scroll to Pop-ups and redirects and click Allow.
For Microsoft Edge:
    1. Navigate to the button next to the URL (site information).
    2. Click Permissions for this site (with a password icon on it).
    3. Scroll to Pop-ups and redirects and click Allow.
For Firefox:
    1. Navigate to the three horizontal lines (on the right side of the toolbar), and click Settings.
    2. Navigate to Privacy and Security on the left-hand side toolbar.
    3. Scroll down to Permissions, and next to Block pop-up windows, click Exceptions....
    4. Type in the URL of the website to allow (which is , with search.html instead of permissions.html). Then click Allow.
This website does not support Internet Explorer.
