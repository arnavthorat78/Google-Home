Version 1.1.0

Accounts

This website now has accounts! This is a very exciting update, as account will give this website a more "personal" feel. Below is a list of the new updates relating to accounts and the description.

The navigation bar now features more links. These include Sign In, Sign Up, and also the account name, which if you click on, will present two buttons - Sign Out and Delete Account.

You can now sign up for an account! The link to go there is on the navbar, with the text Sign Up. There are many benefits for signing up for an account, including...
    - You get nice personalised random greetings when you enter the Home page.
    - You get more functionality with your website.
If you have an account, you can easily log in from any device to access your data. If you do not have an account, the computer will warn you about that. Remember, make sure to log in!

If you want to log out or delete your account, then navigate to your name (on the navbar), then click on it. You will be redirected to a page with a Log Out and a Delete Account button. To log out, press the button, and then click Okay on a confirmation box. You will be logged out.
However, if you want to delete your account, you can navigate to the same page as described above, then click Delete Account. You will be prompted to enter your password, and then your account will be deleted, after clicking Okay on the confirmation box.

Sign up now!

Symbols

What do the symbols and colours mean in front of the updates? Well, the answer is here!

Symbol & Colour Meaning
Red The update is still pending. You will also likely see a button that is disabled.
Yellow The update is going to begin soon. This normally appears when it is going to start pending in a couple of days.
Green The update has just successfully completed. This normally stays for a week after launch.
Grey It has been more than a week since the update has launched. It is advised not to read the description of these, as they might be outdated.
