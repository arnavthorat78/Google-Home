Permissions

Please note that you may have to change the webpage settings for the features to work.
To do this, preform the following steps (these steps are made for Google Chrome only):
    1. Navigate to the button next to the URL (site information).
    2. On the bottom, click Site Settings.
    3. Scroll to Pop-ups and redirects and click Allow.
